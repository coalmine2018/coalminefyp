INTRODUCTION

Our project “Coal Mine Control and Monitoring System” is based on Internet of Things (IoT). It will help to monitor and control the following physical parameters present inside the coal mine i.e., temperature, humidity, smoke, light i ntensity (control through LDR), oxygen level and hazard gases (for example methane and carbon mono-oxide). The concern authorities will monitor these physical parameters through web and mobile application and these parameters will also display on the LCD attached with the project prototype installed inside the coal mine. In case of any emergency situation (for example if the defined parameters will go up and down) the system will generate the alarm and warn the labors to move towards safety.

PURPOSE & SCOPE

The main purpose of our project is that we are going to design a prototype for the safety of the labors who work in the coal mines by detecting the hazard and poisonous gases presents inside the mine. This will make the coal mine a safer place and the major disaster preservation are also effectively solved through our project. In recent years, the coal mine accidents have frequently occurred. According to incomplete statistics, Pakistan's coal imports production accounts for 70%, but the accident casualties is 80%. It causes economic & human losses. Security problem has become the key issues in the coal mine production. Accident prevention is the focus of the current work in the coal mine. Coal mine monitoring system is more and more important to realize the environmental parameters such as hazard gases, temperature, humidity, light intensity and so on. They play an important role in coal mine safety production.
